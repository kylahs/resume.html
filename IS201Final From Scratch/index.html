How I came to LOVE Squirells

Since a young age I was always amazed at the fluffly tailed creature that would walk so easily across my aunts and grandmas fence in Bosie, Idaho. As growing up in Park City, Utah I had not witnessed them in my own yard. This is where the interest first sparked. But then one Sunday evening my older brother wanted to show my family a particular YouTube video not already knowing of my interest in squirells.

The video was the classic Mark Rober squirell maze. It was absolutely AMAZING! Seeing these tiny and sometimes not so tiny creatures complete an obstacle course in order to be rewarded with treats. This is what made me noticed how smart they were and their determination to get to the food. Everything after that is history and I am now an avid squirell lover!
